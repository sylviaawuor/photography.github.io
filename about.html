Shama Kassim is a distinctive Kenyan Baby stylist with a passion for fashion and styling. Her inspiration stemmed from her mother, a designer for close to 40 years, her husband who has been her mentor, motivator and biggest cheerleader and her daughter, Alayna who has been her model since birth.

With more than 4 years’ experience in the Ad agency as well as a Fine Art background, she has mastered the art of creative thinking. Her taste can be described as retro and edgy with a touch of an African style. One can easily describe her as picky, particular and liker of fine things in life.

Her interest lies in capturing the priceless moments a newborn brings to a couple, milestones of a baby and that immeasurable family love. When you walk away, you will feel like you had the best experience and have fallen in love with your photos.
