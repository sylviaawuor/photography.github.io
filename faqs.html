  • Newborn Sessions

    • What's your style of newborn photography?

      I love to work with natural and soft-box lights that is completely safe and friendly to the baby's eyes. I also like to work with light and earthy tones, without an overuse of props, giving your newborn photoshoot a timeless and organic look. I combine standard portraiture with lifestyle to achieve a unique and personal touch to your images.

    • When is the best time to book a session?

      Newborn Photography sessions are best booked in the start of your third trimester. You should book at least 4-6 weeks as only a limited number of newborns are scheduled each month to ensure I am free to accommodate all of them once they arrive.

    • How old should my newborn be for the session?

      Newborn Sessions are best when your infant is within the first 15 days of birth. It is so easy in the excitement of bringing home the little angel to forget or let time pass you by but timing is really critical in scheduling your newborn session. I ask that all my clients to notify me within 3 days of your baby's birth to ensure getting you on the schedule within those first precious days of your new baby's life.

    • Why do i recommend newborn sessions to be held between 5-15 days?

      During 5-15 days, the newborns are the most sleepy and curly so we can get them in those adorable squishy poses. Also, baby acne tends to kick in around the 2nd week and while I will retouch blemishes out of your final images, excessive acne can be difficult to remove entirely. While I really encourage and prefer that we do the session within 2 weeks I realize that sometimes there are reasons that the session cannot be done within this time frame. Should this happen, I will still photograph "older" newborns, however, some of those desired posed/sleepy baby shots may not happen.

    • Where do newborn sessions take place?

      Newborn sessions may take place in my studio or I can come to your home with my studio set up.

    • How long does a session last?

      Sessions typically last 2.5 - 3 hours if we really need a little extra time. However, we do need at most, 30 Mins prior for set-up

    • What is included in the newborn session fee?
      1. Time/talent of the photographer
      2. Use of quality props such as blankets, hats/headbands, props, backdrops and flooring
      3. Carefully posed positioning
      4. Custom edits for jaundice, acne, scratches, blotchy skin
      5. Private online gallery for sharing/purchasing prints
      6. 25 edited high resolution images for download
      7. Professionally designed and printed hardback album
      8. Extra images may be available for purchase
    • How do i book a session when i'm not sure when baby will be born?

      I highly suggest booking your session during your third trimester to guarantee yourself a spot on my newborn list. I can only take a limited amount each month. Obviously many babies are not often born on their due date. So we will just use your due date as a rough estimate. Once your baby is born, you will need to notify me within a couple days after birth so we can schedule your actual date/time.

    • Can i use something i made or purchased to include in the session?

      Sure! Personal items will make your photos all the more special to you. However, I suggest not more than 2 props because I do have an ample supply of props/headbands/hats/sets that I've accumulated that match my style of photography, the backdrops, and work with the flow of the session. My sets are designed to fit and photograph beautifully on newborns. Be sure to try on the prop prior to your session to make sure it fits, often times hats are way too big or tutus will irritate baby. I do have plenty of adorable hats/headbands/sets that are custom designed to fit brand-new babies so don't worry if you don't have anything.

    • Can I Email You My Pinterest Ideas Or Shots I've Seen On Other Sites?

      Yes, requests or inspirations for shots are great! That being said, while I do appreciate ideas and inspiration, I will not replicate or copy someone else's work. I have spent time establishing my own style, so be sure you've looked through my site so you can get a feel for my images. It is also my priority to keep babies safe, so I will not position newborn babies in any manner that will cause them any harm or make them unhappy.

    • Can siblings and parents be in a few shots?

      Yes! We can certainly do a couple of mom/dad and siblings.

    • Do i get to choose which photos will be in my gallery?

      Please trust that I will choose the best images to edit and share in your gallery. I will sift out the unfocused/technically incorrect images. Many shots are duplicates so the best of these sets will be chosen. On occasion if we have a baby that super cooperative I will have many extra shots. If this is the case, these images will show up in your gallery and you will have opportunity to purchase them.

    • Anything else i should know?

      Feed your baby 2 1/2 hours before your session. You can do this by bathing them, keeping them in a diaper and unswaddled, and talking to them. This is REALLY important to end up with a sleepy baby for your session.

      Also, I do ask that you always have an extra set of clothes to go home in, accidents do happen during pictures!

  • Cake Smash

    Time to celebrate the big day with baby's first birthday cake!

    • Where do cake smash sessions take place?

      I love the outdoors and the natural backdrop it provides, so in most cases I really encourage and prefer outdoor sessions.

      I do have some studio backdrop options for an indoor session which can be done in my studio or the client's if we have bad weather or if this is your preference.

    • What is included in the cake smash session?

      I will provide a few appropriate props to coordinate with your theme. Props may include shaggy furs, stools, benches, buckets.

      I will provide your 6″ cake that we coordinate to match your theme. Cake smash sessions are short and to the point. They focus solely on the baby and are not designed to include siblings or other family members. We will do a few clean photos before baby gets messy.

    • Can we include siblings or parents in some photos?

      If you'd like to include some sibling or family photos, we will tack on additional time and there will be an additional fee.

    • What do i need to bring to the cake smash?

      I will provide all props including the cake smash attire in line with your theme. You are also welcome to bring an outfit for clean photos.

      If you have special props that you want to include (teepee, wagons, balloons…) you are certainly welcome to bring them, I do ask that you provide pictures of the props prior to the session.
